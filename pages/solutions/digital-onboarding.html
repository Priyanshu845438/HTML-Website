Where Onboarding Fails in India

A poor first impression doesn't just lose a sign-up; it loses a customer for life. These are the critical friction points we solve.

Complex eKYC & Verification

Clunky, multi-step document uploads for Aadhaar/PAN and slow manual verification processes cause the highest drop-off rates in FinTech and regulated industries.

Poor Mobile Experience

Long forms that are difficult to fill on a small screen, slow loading times on mobile data, and a non-intuitive flow frustrate India's mobile-first user base.

Lack of Clear Guidance

Users are left confused about what to do next, with no clear progress indicators or help, leading to abandonment out of sheer frustration.
